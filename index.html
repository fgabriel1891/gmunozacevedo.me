Hi there! I’m a Quantitative Ecologist and Data Scientist with over 7 years of experience tackling exciting challenges in macroecology, statistical modeling, and climate change research.

While R is my go-to language, I’m also fluent in tools like Python, SQL, and JavaScript. I specialize in making data work for science, with expertise in reproducible technical reporting, CI/CD pipelines, and version control (Git). From processing large-scale biological and environmental datasets to building predictive models and managing big-data workflows, I thrive on turning complex data into actionable insights and impactful publications.

Currently, I’m a Specialist in Advanced Analytics at the Canadian Institute for Health Information (CIHI). I’m also pursuing a PhD in Biology (currently on parental leave) at Concordia University in the Community Ecology and Biogeography Lab.
